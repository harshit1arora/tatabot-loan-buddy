# Underwriting Rules for Tata Capital Loan Processing

## Credit Score Requirements

| Credit Score Range | Decision |
|-------------------|----------|
| < 700 | **REJECT** - Below minimum threshold |
| 700 - 749 | Conditional approval - Additional docs required |
| 750 - 799 | Standard approval |
| 800+ | Premium approval - Fast track |

## Loan Amount Limits

Based on pre-approved limit (PAL):

| Requested Amount | Decision |
|-----------------|----------|
| ≤ PAL | Instant approval (if other criteria met) |
| PAL < Amount ≤ 2×PAL | Conditional - Salary slip required |
| > 2×PAL | **REJECT** - Exceeds maximum limit |

## EMI to Income Ratio

| EMI/Income Ratio | Decision |
|-----------------|----------|
| ≤ 40% | Approve |
| 40% - 50% | Approve with conditions |
| > 50% | **REJECT** - EMI unaffordable |

## Document Requirements

### Mandatory Documents
1. PAN Card
2. Aadhaar Card
3. Address Proof
4. Income Proof (Salary Slip / ITR)

### Conditional Documents
- Bank statements (6 months) - for self-employed
- Property documents - for secured loans
- Business proof - for business loans

## Special Rules

### Salaried Employees
- Minimum 1 year with current employer
- Minimum 2 years total work experience
- Company should be reputed/listed

### Self-Employed
- Minimum 3 years in business
- ITR for last 2 years mandatory
- Business vintage verification required

### Existing Customers
- If existing loan with good repayment: +50 points to credit assessment
- If any default in last 12 months: Auto reject

## Risk Flags (Auto Reject)
- Multiple loan applications in last 30 days
- DPD (Days Past Due) > 90 in last 12 months
- Fraud alert on credit bureau
- CIBIL score unavailable

## Processing Times

| Loan Type | Processing Time |
|-----------|----------------|
| Pre-approved within limit | Instant (2 hours) |
| Standard personal loan | 24-48 hours |
| Conditional approval | 3-5 business days |
| Business loan | 5-7 business days |
